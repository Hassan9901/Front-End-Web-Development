Our mission

Here at iCode, our ambition is to enhance the employability of the promising talent especially who belong to the underprivileged areas. By teaming up with the top talent from the Silicon Valley, we teach, train, mentor and guide these students to enhance their professional skills with the end objective of getting them hired by the top tech companies In this process, these students would be transformed into skilled engineers and meticulous developers. They would not only be ready to be hired but would be skilled enough to become entrepreneurs.
